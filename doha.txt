
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
नहाये धोये क्या भया, जो मन का मैल न जाय । मीन सदा जल में रहै, धोये बास न जाय ॥

MEANING:
Bathing is useless if the mind remains dirty.

TRANSLATION:
Bathing is useless if the mind stays dirty.
====================================
