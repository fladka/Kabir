
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
साधु शब्द समुद्र है, जामें रत्न अपार । मूरख भेद न जानहीं, ढूँढत फिरे किनार ॥

MEANING:
The words of a saint are an ocean filled with gems. Fools don't know the secret and look only on the shore.

TRANSLATION:
A saint's word is an ocean of gems. Fools know not the secret and stay on the shore.
====================================
