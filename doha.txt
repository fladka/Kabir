
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
मन के हारे हार है, मन के जीते जीत । कह कबीर हरि पाइए, मन ही की परतीत ॥

MEANING:
Defeat is in the mind, victory is in the mind. God is found through faith.

TRANSLATION:
Victory and defeat are in the mind. God is found through faith.
====================================
