
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
बुरा जो देखन मैं चला, बुरा न मिलिया कोय । जो दिल खोजा आपना, मुझसे बुरा न कोय ॥

MEANING:
I searched the world for bad people but found none. When I looked inside my own heart, I realized I am the worst of all.

TRANSLATION:
I went searching for the wicked, but found no one. When I searched my own heart, I found no one worse than me.
====================================
