
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
फल कारन सेवा करे, तजे न मन से काम । कह कबीर सेवक नहीं, चहै चोगुना दाम ॥

MEANING:
One who serves for reward and doesn't give up desire is not a servant; he wants four times the price.

TRANSLATION:
He who serves for reward is no servant; he only wants a higher price.
====================================
