
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
हाड़ जलै ज्यों लाकड़ी, केस जलै ज्यों घास । सब जग जलता देखि करि, भये कबीर उदास ॥

MEANING:
Bones burn like wood, hair burns like grass. Seeing the whole world burning (in worldliness), Kabir became sad.

TRANSLATION:
Bones burn like wood, hair like grass. Seeing the world burn, Kabir is saddened.
====================================
