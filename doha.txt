
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
उज्जल पहिरे कापड़ा, पान सुपारी खाय । एकै हरि के नाम बिन, बांधा जमपुर जाय ॥

MEANING:
Fine clothes and food mean nothing without devotion.

TRANSLATION:
Fine clothes are nothing without devotion.
====================================
