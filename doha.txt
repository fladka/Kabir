
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
सबै रसायन मैं किया, प्रेम समान न कोय । रंचक ही में तन तपे, कंचन सा तन होय ॥

MEANING:
I have tried all elixirs, but none is like Love. Even a little makes the body shine like pure gold.

TRANSLATION:
I tried all potions, but none like love. A tiny bit transforms the body into gold.
====================================
