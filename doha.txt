
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा संगत साधु की, ज्यों गंधी की बास । जो कुछ गंधी दे नहीं, तो भी बास सुबास ॥

MEANING:
The company of a saint is like a perfume seller. Even if he gives you nothing, the fragrance stays with you.

TRANSLATION:
Company of a saint is like a perfumer. Even if he gives nothing, the scent remains.
====================================
