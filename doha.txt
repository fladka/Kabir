
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
चाह मिटी चिंता मिटी, मनवा बेपरवाह । जिसको कछू न चाहिए, वो साहन के साह ॥

MEANING:
Ending desire makes you the king of kings.

TRANSLATION:
Ending desire makes you a king.
====================================
