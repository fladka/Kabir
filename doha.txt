
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा गर्व ना कीजिये, काल गहे कर केश । ना जानों कहाँ मारिसी, क्या घर क्या परदेश ॥

MEANING:
Do not be proud; death has you by the hair. You know not where it will strike, whether at home or in a foreign land.

TRANSLATION:
Be not proud, death holds your hair. You know not where it strikes, at home or elsewhere.
====================================
