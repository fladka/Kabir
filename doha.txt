
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
चलती चाकी देख के, दिया कबीरा रोय । दुइ पाटन के बीच में, साबुत बचा न कोय ॥

MEANING:
Nothing stays whole in the duality of the world.

TRANSLATION:
Nothing stays whole in this dual world.
====================================
