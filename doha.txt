
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जब मैं था तब हरि नहीं, अब हरि हैं मैं नाहिं । प्रेम गली अति सांकरी, तामें दो न समाहिं ॥

MEANING:
When I was, God was not. Now God is, and I am not. Love's path is too narrow for two.

TRANSLATION:
When I was, God was not. Now God is, I am not. Love's path is too narrow for two.
====================================
