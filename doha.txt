
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
काल करे सो आज कर, आज करे सो अब । पल में प्रलय होएगी, बहुरि करेगा कब ॥

MEANING:
Do tomorrow's work today, and today's work right now.

TRANSLATION:
Tomorrow's work do today, today's work now. If the moment is lost, how will the work be done?
====================================
