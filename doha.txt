
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जाको राखे साइयां, मारि सके न कोय । बाल न बांका करि सके, जो जग बैरी होय ॥

MEANING:
He whom God protects, no one can kill. Not a hair can be harmed, even if the whole world is an enemy.

TRANSLATION:
Whom God protects, none can slay. Not a hair is harmed if the world is a foe.
====================================
