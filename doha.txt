
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
पाहन पूजे हरि मिले, तो मैं पूजूं पहार । ताते यह चाकी भली, पीस खाय संसार ॥

MEANING:
If worshipping a stone finds God, I'll worship a mountain. Better is the grinding stone that feeds the world.

TRANSLATION:
If stones find God, I'll worship a hill. Better is the millstone that feeds the world.
====================================
