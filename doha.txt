
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
मंगन मरण समान है, मति कोउ मांगो भीख । मंगन से तो मरन भला, यह सतगुरु की सीख ॥

MEANING:
Begging is like death. It is better to die than to beg.

TRANSLATION:
Begging is like death. Better to die than beg.
====================================
