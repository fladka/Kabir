
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
सोना सज्जन साधु जन, टूटि जुरै सौ बार । दुर्जन कुम्भ कुम्हार के, एकै धका दरार ॥

MEANING:
Good people join even if broken. The wicked shatter forever.

TRANSLATION:
Good people join; the wicked shatter forever.
====================================
