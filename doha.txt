
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
माटी कहे कुम्हार से, तु क्या रौंदे मोय । एक दिन ऐसा आएगा, मैं रौंदूगी तोय ॥

MEANING:
Clay says to the potter: Why do you trample me? One day I will trample you.

TRANSLATION:
Clay tells the potter: You trample me now, but one day I will trample you.
====================================
