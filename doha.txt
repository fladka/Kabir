
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा यहु तन जात है, सकै तो लेहु बहोरि । जा घट प्रीति न राम की, सो घट गया अकारि ॥

MEANING:
This body is fading. Without devotion, life is wasted.

TRANSLATION:
This body is fading. Without devotion, life is in vain.
====================================
