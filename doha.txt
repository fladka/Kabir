
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
पोथी पढ़ि पढ़ि जग मुआ, पंडित भया न कोय । ढाई आखर प्रेम का, पढ़े सो पंडित होय ॥

MEANING:
Reading books the world died, no one became wise. He who reads 'Love' becomes wise.

TRANSLATION:
Books didn't make the world wise. He who reads 'Love' is truly wise.
====================================
