
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
चिंता से चतुराई घटे, दुख से घटे शरीर । पाप से लक्ष्मी घटे, कह गए दास कबीर ॥

MEANING:
Worry reduces cleverness, sorrow weakens the body. Sin reduces wealth.

TRANSLATION:
Worry dulls the mind, sorrow hurts the body. Sin takes wealth away.
====================================
