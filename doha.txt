
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर मानुष जन्म दुर्लभ है, देह न बारम्बार । तरुवर से पत्ता झड़ गया, बहुरि न लागे डार ॥

MEANING:
Human life is rare; this body comes but once. Once a leaf falls from a tree, it never rejoins the branch.

TRANSLATION:
Life is rare, the body comes once. A fallen leaf never returns to the branch.
====================================
