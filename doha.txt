
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
सतगुरु हमसों रीझि करि, एक कह्या प्रसङ्ग । बरस्या बादल प्रेम का, भीजि गया सब अङ्ग ॥

MEANING:
The Guru's word rained love and soaked my soul.

TRANSLATION:
The Guru's word soaked my entire soul in love.
====================================
