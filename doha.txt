
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
धीरे-धीरे रे मना, धीरे सब कुछ होय । माली सींचे सौ घड़ा, ॠतु आए फल होय ॥

MEANING:
Slowly, oh mind, everything happens slowly. The gardener may water with a hundred pots, but fruit only comes in its season.

TRANSLATION:
Slowly my mind, slowly everything happens. The gardener waters a hundred pots, but fruit arrives only in its season.
====================================
