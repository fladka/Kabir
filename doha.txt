
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
पोथी पढ़ि पढ़ि जग मुआ, पंडित भया न कोय । ढाई आखर प्रेम का, पढ़े सो पंडित होय ॥

MEANING:
Reading books, the world died, no one became wise. One who reads the two and a half letters of 'Love' becomes wise.

TRANSLATION:
Reading books the world died, none became a scholar. He who reads the two and a half letters of Love, becomes a scholar.
====================================
