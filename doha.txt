
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जग में बैरी कोई नहीं, जो मन शीतल होय । यह आपा तो डारि दे, दया करे सब कोय ॥

MEANING:
No one is your enemy if your mind is calm. If you drop your ego, everyone will show you compassion.

TRANSLATION:
No one is your foe if your mind is cool. Drop your ego, and the world will be kind to you.
====================================
