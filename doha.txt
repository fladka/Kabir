
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
ऐसी बानी बोलिए, मन का आपा खोय । औरन को शीतल करे, आपहुं शीतल होय ॥

MEANING:
Speak such words that you lose your ego. Words that cool the listener and cool yourself.

TRANSLATION:
Speak such words that you lose your ego. It cools others and cools yourself too.
====================================
