
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर आपा मेटि करि, हरि भजि तजि अभिमान । जिहि घट दया न संचरे, सो घट जान मसान ॥

MEANING:
Kabir, erase your ego and worship God, leaving pride. A heart without compassion is a cremation ground.

TRANSLATION:
Kabir, kill the ego and pray. A heart without mercy is a graveyard.
====================================
