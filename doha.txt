
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
दुर्लभ मानुष जन्म है, देह न बारम्बार । तरुवर ज्यों पत्ती झड़े, बहुरि न लागे डार ॥

MEANING:
Human birth is rare; this body is not gained again and again. Like a leaf falls from a tree and never returns to the branch.

TRANSLATION:
Human life is rare, it comes not again. Like a fallen leaf that never returns to the branch.
====================================
