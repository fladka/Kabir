
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
रात गंवाई सोय के, दिवस गंवाया खाय । हीरा जनम अमोल था, कौड़ी बदले जाय ॥

MEANING:
The night was lost in sleep, the day in eating. This diamond-like life was priceless, but it's going for a penny.

TRANSLATION:
Night lost to sleep, day to food. This priceless life is sold for a penny.
====================================
