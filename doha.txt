
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
तिनका कबहुं ना निंदिये, जो पाँवन तर होय । कबहुं उड़ ऑंखिन पड़े, पीर घनेरी होय ॥

MEANING:
Never mock the small; it can cause great pain.

TRANSLATION:
Never mock the small; it causes great pain.
====================================
