
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जाति न पूछो साधु की, पूछ लीजिये ज्ञान । मोल करो तलवार का, पड़ा रहन दो म्यान ॥

MEANING:
Don't ask a saint's caste, ask for his wisdom. Price the sword, leave the sheath.

TRANSLATION:
Ask wisdom, not caste. Value the sword, not the sheath.
====================================
