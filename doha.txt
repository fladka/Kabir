
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
चिंता ऐसी डाकिनी, काटि कलेजा खाय । बैद बिचारा क्या करे, कहाँ तक दवा लगाय ॥

MEANING:
Worry is such a witch that she eats the heart. What can the poor doctor do? How far can medicine reach?

TRANSLATION:
Worry is a witch that eats the heart. What can a doctor do? Medicine cannot reach it.
====================================
