
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
गुरु गोविंद दोऊ खड़े, काके लागूं पाँय । बलिहारी गुरु आपने, गोविंद दियो बताय ॥

MEANING:
Both Guru and God are standing before me, whose feet should I touch first? I bow to my Guru.

TRANSLATION:
Guru and God both are standing, whose feet should I touch? I surrender to my Guru who introduced me to God.
====================================
