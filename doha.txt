
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
माया दीपक नर पतंग, भ्रमि भ्रमि इवैं पड़ंत । कह कबीर गुरु ज्ञान थे, एक आध उबरंत ॥

MEANING:
Maya is the flame and man the moth. Guru's wisdom saves a few.

TRANSLATION:
Maya is the flame, man the moth. Guru's wisdom saves some.
====================================
