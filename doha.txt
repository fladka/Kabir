
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
माला फेरत जुग भया, फिरा न मन का फेर । कर का मनका डारि दे, मन का मनका फेर ॥

MEANING:
A whole age passed turning beads, but the mind didn't turn. Drop the wooden beads and turn the beads of the heart.

TRANSLATION:
Years passed turning beads, the mind didn't change. Drop the beads, turn the heart.
====================================
