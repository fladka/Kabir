
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जा घट प्रेम न संचरे, सो घट जान मसान । जैसे खाल लुहार की, सांस लेत बिनु प्रान ॥

MEANING:
A heart without love is like a cremation ground. Like the blacksmith's bellows, it breathes without life.

TRANSLATION:
A heart without love is a graveyard. It breathes like bellows, but has no life.
====================================
