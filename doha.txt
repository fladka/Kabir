
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
प्रेम पियाला जो पिये, शीश दक्षिणा देय । लोभी शीश न दे सके, नाम प्रेम का लेय ॥

MEANING:
He who drinks the cup of love gives his head as a gift. The greedy cannot give their head but still speak of love.

TRANSLATION:
He who drinks love's cup gives his life. The greedy cannot, yet they speak of love.
====================================
