
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
ज्यों नैनों में पुतली, त्यों मालिक घट माहिं । मूरख लोग न जानहीं, बाहर ढूँढन जाहिं ॥

MEANING:
As the pupil is in the eye, the Lord is within the body. Fools know it not and search outside.

TRANSLATION:
As the pupil is in the eye, God is within. Fools seek Him outside.
====================================
