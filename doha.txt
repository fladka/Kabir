
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा संगत साधु की, ज्यों गंधी की बास । जो कुछ गंधी दे नहीं, तो भी बास सुबास ॥

MEANING:
Good company leaves its scent even without words.

TRANSLATION:
Good company leaves its scent.
====================================
