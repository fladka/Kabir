
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर सोई सुरमा, जो मन सों खेलै जाय । पंच चपेटा मारि करि, मन को वश में लाय ॥

MEANING:
The true hero is one who conquers his own mind.

TRANSLATION:
The true hero conquers his own mind.
====================================
