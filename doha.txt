
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा गरब न कीजिये, ऊँचा देखि आवास । काल परों भुईं लेटना, ऊपर जमसी घास ॥

MEANING:
Do not be proud of your high mansion. Tomorrow you will lie in the dust, and grass will grow over you.

TRANSLATION:
Be not proud of your high home. Tomorrow you lie in dust, covered in grass.
====================================
