
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर दीवा तेल बिन, बाती बिनु जरि जाय । साँझ सकारे राम कहि, कदे न बुझि जाय ॥

MEANING:
Divine remembrance is a light that never goes out.

TRANSLATION:
Divine remembrance is a light that never dies.
====================================
