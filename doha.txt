
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
काया कांची कुंभ है, लिये फिरे था साथ । टपका लागा फूटि गया, कछू न आया हाथ ॥

MEANING:
The body is a fragile clay pot that shatters instantly.

TRANSLATION:
The body is a fragile clay pot.
====================================
