
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जैसे तिल में तेल है, ज्यों चकमक में आग । तेरा साईं तुझ में है, तू जाग सके तो जाग ॥

MEANING:
Just as oil is inside the sesame seed, and fire is in the flint stone. Your God is inside you, wake up if you can.

TRANSLATION:
Like oil in sesame, like fire in flint. Your Lord is within you, awake if you can awake.
====================================
