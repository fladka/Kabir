
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा संगत साधु की, कदे न निरफल होय । लोहा कंचन होत है, जो पारस परसे कोय ॥

MEANING:
Company with a saint never goes in vain. Just as iron turns to gold when it touches the philosopher's stone.

TRANSLATION:
Company of a saint is never wasted. Iron turns to gold by the touch of the stone.
====================================
