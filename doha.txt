
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
लूटि सके तो लूट ले, राम नाम की लूट । पाछे ही पछताओगे, प्राण जाहिंगे छूटि ॥

MEANING:
Gather the name of God while you can. You will regret it later when life leaves your body.

TRANSLATION:
Gather God's name now. You will regret it when life departs.
====================================
