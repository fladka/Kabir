
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर तन पंछी भया, जहाँ मन तहाँ उडि जाय । जो जैसी संगति करै, सो तैसा फल खाय ॥

MEANING:
The body follows the mind. You reap the fruit of your company.

TRANSLATION:
The body follows the mind. You reap what you sow in company.
====================================
