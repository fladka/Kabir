
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर सोई पीर है, जो जाने पर पीर । जो पर पीर न जानई, सो काफिर बेपीर ॥

MEANING:
He is a true saint who knows others' pain. He who does not is heartless.

TRANSLATION:
A true saint feels others' pain. He who doesn't is without a soul.
====================================
