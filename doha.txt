
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर सो धन संचिये, जो आगै को होय । सीस चढ़ाये पोटली, ले जात न देख्या कोय ॥

MEANING:
Save the wealth that lasts beyond death.

TRANSLATION:
Save the wealth that lasts beyond the grave.
====================================
