
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
माटी कहे कुम्हार से, तु क्या रौंदे मोय । एक दिन ऐसा आएगा, मैं रौंदूगी तोय ॥

MEANING:
The clay says to the potter, 'Why do you trample me? A day will come when I will trample you.'

TRANSLATION:
Clay says to the potter, why do you knead me? A day will come when I will knead you.
====================================
