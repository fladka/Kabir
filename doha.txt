
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर दर्शन साधु के, बड़े भाग से होय । जो कछु किया सोई मिला, अब कछु और न कोय ॥

MEANING:
Meeting a saint happens through great fortune. What you did is what you got; there is nothing else now.

TRANSLATION:
Meeting a saint is great luck. You get what you gave; nothing more remains.
====================================
