
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जैसी प्रीति कुटुम्ब की, तैसी हरि सो होय । चला जाय वैकुण्ठ को, पल्ला न पकड़े कोय ॥

MEANING:
If your love for God was like your love for family, you would reach heaven easily.

TRANSLATION:
Love God like you love family, and heaven is yours.
====================================
