
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
साईं इतना दीजिये, जा मे कुटुम समाय । मैं भी भूखा न रहूँ, साधु ना भूखा जाय ॥

MEANING:
Lord, give me enough to sustain my family and guests.

TRANSLATION:
Lord, give me enough for family and guests.
====================================
