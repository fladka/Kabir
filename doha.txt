
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
चाह मिटी चिंता मिटी, मनवा बेपरवाह । जिसको कछू न चाहिए, वो साहन के साह ॥

MEANING:
Desire ended, worry ended, the mind became carefree. He who wants nothing is the king of kings.

TRANSLATION:
Desire and worry gone, the mind is free. He who wants nothing is the king of kings.
====================================
