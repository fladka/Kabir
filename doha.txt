
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
दुख में सुमिरन सब करे, सुख में करै न कोय । जो सुख में सुमिरन करे, दुख काहे को होय ॥

MEANING:
Everyone remembers God in sorrow, no one remembers in joy. If one remembers God in joy, why would sorrow ever come?

TRANSLATION:
In sorrow everyone remembers Him, in joy no one does. If one remembers Him in joy, why would sorrow come?
====================================
