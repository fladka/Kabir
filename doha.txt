
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
चिंता ऐसी डाकिनी, काटि कलेजा खाय । बैद बिचारा क्या करे, कहाँ तक दवा लगाय ॥

MEANING:
Worry eats the heart; no medicine can reach it.

TRANSLATION:
Worry eats the heart; no medicine reaches it.
====================================
