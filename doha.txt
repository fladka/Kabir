
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा तेलिन के बैल को, घर ही कोस पचास । सवेरे उठा फिर वहीं, साँझ हुई तो पास ॥

MEANING:
The oilman's bull travels fifty miles but stays in the same place. He starts and ends in the same spot.

TRANSLATION:
The oilman's bull walks miles but stays put. He ends where he began.
====================================
