
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
माली आवत देख के, कलियन करे पुकार । फूले-फूले चुन लिये, काल्हि हमारी बार ॥

MEANING:
Seeing the gardener coming, the buds cry out. He picked all the flowers today; tomorrow it will be our turn.

TRANSLATION:
Seeing the gardener, the buds cry: He picked the flowers today, tomorrow is our turn.
====================================
