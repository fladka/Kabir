
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कामी क्रोधी लालची, इनसे भक्ति न होय । भक्ति करे कोइ सूरमा, जाति वरन कुल खोय ॥

MEANING:
The greedy and angry cannot truly devote themselves.

TRANSLATION:
The greedy and angry cannot truly devote.
====================================
