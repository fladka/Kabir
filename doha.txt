
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
साधु ऐसा चाहिये, जैसा सूप सुभाय । सार-सार को गहि रहै, थोथा देइ उड़ाय ॥

MEANING:
A saint should be like a winnowing basket. It keeps the essence and blows away the hollow chaff.

TRANSLATION:
A saint should be like a winnowing fan. Keep the truth, blow away the false.
====================================
