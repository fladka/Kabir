
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
माली आवत देख के, कलियन करे पुकार । फूले-फूले चुन लिये, काल्हि हमारी बार ॥

MEANING:
Time waits for no one; our turn is coming.

TRANSLATION:
Time waits for none; our turn comes.
====================================
