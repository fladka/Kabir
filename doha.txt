
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जो तोको कांटा बुवै, ताहि बोय तू फूल । तोको फूल के फूल हैं, बाको है तिरशूल ॥

MEANING:
If someone sows thorns for you, sow flowers for them. You will get flowers, and they will get thorns.

TRANSLATION:
If one sows thorns, sow flowers. You get flowers, they get thorns.
====================================
