
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
पढ़ि पढ़ि के पत्थर भये, लिखि लिखि भये जु ईंट । कह कबीर वा प्रेम की, एको मिली न छींट ॥

MEANING:
Reading without love makes one like a stone.

TRANSLATION:
Endless reading without love makes one like a stone.
====================================
