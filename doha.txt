
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
प्रेम बिना जो जप तपै, सो सब निष्फल जाय । कबीर सोई भक्त है, जाके हिरदै प्रेम समाय ॥

MEANING:
Prayers and penance without love are fruitless.

TRANSLATION:
Prayers without love are in vain.
====================================
