
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
पाँच पहर धंधे गया, तीन पहर गया सोय । एको घड़ी न सिमिरया, तो जम की मार क्यूं न होय ॥

MEANING:
Work and sleep took your time. Forgetting God leads to death.

TRANSLATION:
Work and sleep took your time. Forgetting God leads to certain death.
====================================
