
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कस्तूरी कुंडली बसै, मृग ढूँढै बन माहिं । ज्यों घट-घट राम हैं, दुनिया देखै नाहिं ॥

MEANING:
God is within every heart, yet the world seeks Him outside.

TRANSLATION:
God is within, yet the world seeks Him outside.
====================================
