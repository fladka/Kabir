
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर संगत साधु की, बेगि करीजै जाय । दुरमति दूरि गंवाइ सी, देशी सुमति बताय ॥

MEANING:
Seek a saint's company to remove bad thoughts and gain wisdom.

TRANSLATION:
Seek a saint's company to drive away evil thoughts.
====================================
