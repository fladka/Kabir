
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा तेरी झोंपड़ी, गलकटियन के पास । जो करेंगे सो भरेंगे, तुम क्यों भये उदास ॥

MEANING:
You reap what you sow; don't worry about others' actions.

TRANSLATION:
You reap what you sow; don't worry about others.
====================================
