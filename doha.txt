
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीरा यहु घर प्रेम का, खाला का घर नाहिं । सीस उतारे हाथि करि, सो पैठे घर माहिं ॥

MEANING:
This is the house of love. Sacrifice ego to enter.

TRANSLATION:
This is the home of love. Sacrifice your ego to enter.
====================================
