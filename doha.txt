
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर कहता हूँ कहि जात हूँ, कहूँ जो ठोक बजाय । कुआँ पास है आपणे, तू क्यों प्यासा जाय ॥

MEANING:
The source of wisdom is near; don't remain thirsty.

TRANSLATION:
Wisdom is near; don't remain thirsty.
====================================
