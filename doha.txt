
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
एक ही साधे सब सधे, सब साधे सब जाय । माली सींचे मूल को, फूले फले अघाय ॥

MEANING:
Master one and master all. Water the root to get fruit.

TRANSLATION:
Master one to gain all. Water the root to flourish.
====================================
