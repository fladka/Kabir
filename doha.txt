
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
बोली एक अनमोल है, जो कोई बोले जानि । हिये तराजू तौलि के, तब मुख बाहर आनि ॥

MEANING:
Words are priceless; weigh them in your heart.

TRANSLATION:
Weigh words in your heart before speaking.
====================================
