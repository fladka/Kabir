
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
जग में रहिबे को कछू, ठौर ठिकाना नाहिं । कबीर राम नाम बिन, जम के फंदे माहिं ॥

MEANING:
Nothing is permanent. Without devotion, death traps you.

TRANSLATION:
Nothing is permanent. Without devotion, you are trapped by death.
====================================
