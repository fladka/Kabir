
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर नौबत आपणी, दिन दस लेहु बजाय । यहु पुर पटन यहु गली, बहुरि न देखहु आय ॥

MEANING:
Live fully now. You won't return to these streets again.

TRANSLATION:
Live your life fully now. You won't see these streets again.
====================================
