
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कहता हूँ कहि जात हूँ, कहूँ जो ठोक बजाय । कुआँ पास है आपणे, तू क्यों प्यासा जाय ॥

MEANING:
I say it again and again, and I say it with certainty. The well is near you; why do you remain thirsty?

TRANSLATION:
I tell you truly, the well is right here. Why do you still wander with thirst?
====================================
