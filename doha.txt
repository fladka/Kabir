
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
बड़ा हुआ तो क्या हुआ, जैसे पेड़ खजूर । पंथी को छाया नहीं, फल लागे अति दूर ॥

MEANING:
What is the use of being big like a date tree? It gives no shade to travelers and its fruits are too high to reach.

TRANSLATION:
What if you are big, like the date palm tree. No shade for the traveler, and fruit grows too far.
====================================
