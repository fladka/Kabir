
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
निंदक नियरे राखिए, ऑंगन कुटी छवाय । बिन पानी, साबुन बिना, निर्मल करे सुभाय ॥

MEANING:
Keep your critics close. Without water or soap, they cleanse your character.

TRANSLATION:
Keep critics close. They clean your nature without soap or water.
====================================
