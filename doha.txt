
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
बपु बपुरो बुड़न डरो, रहा किनारे बैठ । जिन खोजा तिन पाइया, गहरे पानी पैठ ॥

MEANING:
Timidity stays on the shore. Seekers find treasures in the deep.

TRANSLATION:
The timid stay on the bank. Seekers find treasures in the deep water.
====================================
