
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
कबीर कलजुग आइया, करम कथै सब कोय । जेहि घट दया न संचरे, सो कलिहारी होय ॥

MEANING:
A heart without mercy is lost in this dark age.

TRANSLATION:
A heart without mercy is truly lost in this dark age.
====================================
