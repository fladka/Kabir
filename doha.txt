
====================================
           KABIR DOHA            
====================================
साईं से लगन लगाये रख, फल की इच्छा त्याग । जब समय आएगा तेरा, बदल जाएगा भाग ॥

MEANING:
Keep your devotion to the Lord and give up the desire for fruits. When your time comes, your fate will change.

TRANSLATION:
Stay devoted to the Lord, forget the reward. When the time is right, your luck will turn.
====================================
